I was born in 1980 and am currently unmarried. For many years, I successfully ran two businesses in Russia, which required significant time and energy. Although I've long been passionate about web development—primarily building websites with Joomla—I couldn't fully pursue it due to my demanding business responsibilities. Given my strong opposition to the ongoing war and the current political regime in Russia, I've decided to shift my professional path. I’ve now fully committed myself to studying full-stack web development, aiming to transform this long-standing interest into a professional career. My goal is to become a skilled full-stack developer, leveraging technology to build innovative solutions and achieve financial independence outside my existing businesses. Apart from work, I enjoy sports, traveling, and surrounding myself with great people.
